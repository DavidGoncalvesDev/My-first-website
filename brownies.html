How to Cook Brownies

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8 inch baking pan.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt 1/2 cup of unsalted butter.
  3. Whisk 1 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract into the melted butter.
  4. Beat in 2 large eggs until the mixture is well combined.
  5. Fold in 1/3 cup of cocoa powder, 1/2 cup of flour, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  8. Let the brownies cool completely before cutting.
